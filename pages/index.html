I'm a postdoctoral research assistant at the Torr Vision Group in the Department of Engineering Science at the University of Oxford, working with Phil Torr

I got my doctoral degree at the Center for the Computational Study of Cognition, Communication, Control, and Perception, with Jeffrey Mark Siskind at Purdue University, and was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the Computation and Cognition Lab in the Department of Psychology at Stanford, working with Noah Goodman.

My research broadly involves the confluence of computer vision, robotics, natural-language processing, cognitive science, and elements of cognitive neuroscience, leading towards a central research goal to better understand perception and cognition.
